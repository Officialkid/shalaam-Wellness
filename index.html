About Clare

Clare Wamaitha

Counselling Psychologist & Founder, Shalam Wellness

Clare is a Counselling Psychologist registered with the Counsellors and Psychologists Board of Kenya. She holds a Diploma and BA in Counselling Psychology, complemented by certifications in Psychological First Aid, Trauma-Informed Care, and Mindfulness & Self-Compassion.

With over three years of experience working with children, adolescents, youth and adults, Clare offers compassionate, evidence-based psychological support across diverse life challenges. Her approach integrates Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) with a person-centred framework.

Clare is deeply committed to creating a safe, non-judgmental space where clients can openly explore their concerns, heal from past experiences, and build healthier, more fulfilling ways of living.
